Why We're Here





Rehabilitation can be laborious and frustrating -- our mission is to make it fun and engaging, so that patients can enjoy the process and feel motivation to work hard.

We promise to listen carefully and thoughtfully to all of our patients in order to provide a device that meets the needs of the entire range of recovering stroke patients.

Our Core Values

Accountability


We pledge to meet our patients' demands for product refinement and customer service as best as we can.

Accessibility


We will listen to concerns of those who use our product and stay accessible to a wide range of patients.

Empathy


We are driven by the understanding that physical therapy is laborious/tedious for patients.

Adaptability


We will continue to research novel ways to improve our device.
