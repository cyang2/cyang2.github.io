Why We're Here

Rehabilitation can be laborious and frustrating -- our mission is to make it fun and engaging, so that patients can enjoy the process and feel motivation to work hard.

We promise to listen carefully and thoughtfully to all of our patients in order to provide a device that meets the needs of the entire range of recovering stroke patients.
