How It Works:



Stroke recovery as simple as playing a game.

One of the most effective methods of physical rehabilitation is “Task Specific Training (TST),” which involves repetition of tasks that mimic regular daily actions.

The Track Trainer is a gamified device for recovering stroke patients that makes rehabilitation fun. Users guide the handle through a track, completing TST challenges along the way, and race to the finish line as fast as they can!
