Simple, Effective, Affordable

The Track Trainer is a fun, game-like rehabilitation device for hands, arms and shoulders.

Patients can easily see improvement over the course of their therapy by recording how fast they complete each lap of the track.

Best of all, the Track Trainer’s simple design makes it one of the most affordable rehabilitation devices in the market.
